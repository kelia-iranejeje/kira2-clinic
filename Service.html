Our Services
  • Emergency Care – Immediate treatment for critical conditions like accidents, heart attacks, and strokes.
  • Surgery – Both minor and major procedures, including transplants, orthopedic surgeries, and general operations.
  • Maternity Services – Care for pregnant women, childbirth, and neonatal care for newborns.
  • Pharmacy Services – Dispensing medications prescribed by doctors.
  • Diagnostic Services – Laboratory tests, X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) – Specialized care for critically ill patients.
  • Outpatient Services – Consultations, minor treatments, and follow-ups without hospital admission.
  • Rehabilitation Services – Physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy for recovery.
  • Mental Health Services – Counseling, psychiatric care, and therapy sessions.
  • Departments – Cardiology, oncology, pediatrics, neurology, and other specialized medical fields.
